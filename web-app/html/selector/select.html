Guide

* Best is a colour photograph that clearly shows the profile of object and its primary or most interesting imagery.
* There are some cases where a profile isn't the most representative image, e.g. in the case of a plate or saucer with a decorated tondo, where a top view showing the tondo and/or handles would be preferable.
* Choose a picture that shows the single object.
* While colour is good prioritize crisp b/w pictures over blurry colour.
* Please email amy (a.c.smith@reading.ac.uk) about any objects that seem to be allocated to the wrong object.
